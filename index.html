About Us

Gefyra (γέφυρα): Greek; noun; bridge, structure over river, etc.

Gefyra AI is a medical AI solutions spin off built up from members of the lab of precision psychiatry at the department of psychiatry and psychotherapy at the Ludwig Maximilians University Munich. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology to optimise prevention, prediction, diagnosis and treatment optimisation in healthcare and medical research.

Our team boasts lending and innovative research expertise in mental health AI, extensive experience in developing AI models as functional applications in clinical environments, and a close proximity to both clinicians and patients. With years of experience in research, machine learning, and software development, we aim to bridge the gap between researchers, clinicians, pharmaceuticals, healthcare services and patients, providing AI-assisted solutions for our clients in every step from ideation to product development.

Services

Our iterative research and development process at a glance

Project Scoping

First, we need to fully understand your project's goals and objectives. Based on that, we can help to guide and refine the project's direction while defining what requirements your AI solution has to fulfill.

Solutions Research

We use our academic research expertise to identify the potential technologies and solutions for your project to help you achieve the project goal.

Model Development & Validation

We will select the solution with the optimal potential impact. We evaluate the potential of each approach identified during the solutions research phase. We select the approach that holds the most promise for success before the prototype development phase.

Prototyping & Product Development

We will help you build a user-focused software solution that brings your idea to life. We can turn your AI and machine learning models into a digital user experience which can be integrated into your value creating chain.
